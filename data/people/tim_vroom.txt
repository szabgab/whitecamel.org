Tim Vroom is the creator of <a href="http://www.perlmonks.org/">PerlMonks</a>, 
a popular community website 
for Perl users. PerlMonks, a website designed for the 
"seekers of Perl wisdom", is a welcoming place for participants 
to ask questions, post observations, and submit code for the 
community to discuss and use. Tim has been been running and 
improving PerlMonks since 1999, and has made it a very 
important resource for Perl users.
