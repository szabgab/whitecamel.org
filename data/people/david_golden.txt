You've run across David Golden's work behind the scenes of the Perl
community, and probably recognize him as "xdg" . He could have
received a White Camel in any category. He's a constant force in
establishing consensus for the Perl toolchain, the QA workshops, and
many other social constructs that make everything work together.
Besides that technical work, he's also one of the organizers of the
<a href="https://www.meetup.com/The-New-York-Perl-Meetup-Group/">New York
Perl mongers</a>.
