Graham Barr is the creator of the popular search interface for 
the Comprehensive Perl Archive Network (CPAN). Graham has been 
quietly running the 
<a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20180624041928/http://search.cpan.org/" class="web-archive">search.cpan.org</a>* website for years, providing 
a service that thousands of Perl users rely upon every day. 
Graham recently released an updated interface to this website, 
making it even more useful.

<br/><br/>
<i>* search.cpan.org was replaced by <a href="https://metacpan.org/">MetaCPAN</a> on June 25, 2018</i>
